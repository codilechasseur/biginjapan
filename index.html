Getting around

Grab Suica / ICOCA IC cards (or add Suica to Apple/Google Wallet) and tap onto everything. Google Maps transit directions are gospel here.

Bullet trains

A JR Pass rarely pays off for this trip — book point-to-point Nozomi tickets and reserve seats. Buy an ekiben bento for the ride.

Money

Carry some cash; many small spots are cash-only. 7-Bank ATMs in every konbini take foreign cards.

Travelling with a 9-yo

One big thing + one calm thing per day. Forward luggage city-to-city with takkyubin so no one drags suitcases up station stairs.
